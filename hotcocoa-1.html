Hot Cocoa



WHAT IS IT?

Organo™ Gourmet Hot Cocoa mix provides a comforting warm and silky hot cocoa drink mix that is rich in flavour. It can be enjoyed hot or iced for a delicious cocoa treat.

✓ Perfect for the whole family, child friendly.

✓ Caffeine free

✓ Features organic Ganoderma lucidum

Product packaging may vary depending on availability

KEY INGREDIENTS

Cocoa – The Latin name for cocoa—Theobroma—literally means, “Food of the gods.” Fibre – each OG Gourmet Hot Cocoa provides 2.8gr of fibre per serving. Ganoderma – also known as the red Reishi mushroom, this mild flavour adds a note of depth to the cocoa flavour. Ganoderma has been used in traditional Asian culture for thousands of years. The name Ganoderma means “spiritual potency”, while the Japanese name Reishi can be translated as “King of Herbs”. Malt Extract – Malt extract helps give the OG Gourmet Hot Cocoa an ‘old fashioned’ cocoa flavour. Organo™ Gourmet Hot Cocoa provides cocoa with ganoderma and naturally contains fibre.


HOW TO USE

Empty 1 sachet into your favourite cup, fill with hot water, stir and enjoy! You can also add ice for a creamy cool cocoa treat.
