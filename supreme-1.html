Café Supreme



WHAT IS IT?

Organo™ Gourmet Café Supreme is a light and mild blend of coffee, with creamy flavor and sweetness. It’s an ideal blend for early in the morning or a mid-day pick me up.

✓ Contains ginseng

✓ Features organic Ganoderma lucidum

KEY INGREDIENTS

Coffee – one of the most popular beverages in the world. Organo™ uses a strong, easy to mix instant coffee that is bold and flavorful. Portable and convenient to carry, for on the go instant coffee benefits. Fiber – each Café Supreme has 2 gr of fiber. Organo™ Café Supreme provides coffee with Ganoderma, and naturally contains fiber. Ganoderma lucidum– also known as the red Reishi mushroom, this mild flavor adds a note of depth to the coffee flavor and brings its own benefits. Ganoderma mushrooms provide triterpenoids and amino acids. Ganoderma mushrooms are ‘adaptogens’. Ganoderma lucidum has been used in traditional Asian culture for thousands of years. The name Ganoderma lucidum means “spiritual potency”, while the Japanese name Reishi can be translated as “King of Herbs”. Ginseng – imparts a slight spicy note and naturally contains phytochemicals. Over 5,000 years ago, in the mountains of Manchuria, China, Panax ginseng was commonly used for its rejuvenating powers Eurycoma longifolia Jack Powder – The herb name for what is more commonly known as Tongkat Ali, a Malaysian Ginseng, or Longjack. Creamer – adds a hint of sweetness and creamy flavor.



HOW TO USE IT

Empty 1 sachet into your favorite cup, fill with hot water, stir and enjoy! You can also add ice for a cool coffee treat.
